ASIS&T @ Ualbany

Welcome to the ASIS&T homepage for the University at Albany. ASIS&T stands the Association for Information Science and Technology. Here we are dedicated to providing the community with several resources ranging from topics in the information sciences and computer software, to hosting hackathons and Open Source Festivals. We look to provide people with the opportunties to become connected to many business in the field of informatics and big data. We also advertise companies as a venue for increased awareness in the University of Albany as well as the capital district of New York.
Vision
Our vision is to herald a new information professionalism in a world where the information sciences is of central importance to personal, social, political, and economic progress by: Advancing knowledge about several areas in the information sciences, its properties and its use; Providing workshops on databases, progamming languages, algorithmic concepts and modern technologies; nurturing new perspectives, interests, and ideas and increasing public awareness of the information sciences and technologies and their benefits to society.
@Ualbany

Here at the University at Albany, the ASIS&T organization has been providing the community with many enriching opportunities for advancement in the Information and Computer Sciences. Our growing organization shows sucess as we plan to work with several businesses in the information science fields as well as our local resources within the community at the University to provide greater access to resources, innovation, technology and careers in all aspects of the field.
